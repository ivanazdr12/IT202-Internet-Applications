Fun Facts About Show

  1. This show was NBC's second trial to making a Friday Night Lights series
  2. Most students at Dillon High were not teenagers
  3. Show was filmed in Austin
  4. Uniforms and some footage from the games were taken from the Pflugerville Panthers
  5. Some actors knew nothing about football
  6. There were no rehearsals during film recording
